About the Author

Welcome to my personal corner. I am **Christine Joy Alutaya**, a graduate of St. Vincent de Ferrer College of Camarin Inc. [cite: 14], where I earned my degree in English Education with the highest honor, **Summa Cum Laude**. My journey as a Licensed Professional Teacher [cite: 17] has always been intertwined with my love for the written word.


Writing is my way of breathing. Whether it's the structured beauty of a sonnet or the raw honesty of free verse, I find that language is the only tool capable of capturing the sunlight between the shadows. This page isn't just a portfolio; it's a piece of my heart, organized by date and mood.
