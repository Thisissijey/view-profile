Ink & Echoes

A collection of ten distinct verses, written from the soul.

The First Bell

The chalk dust dances in the morning light, A silent witness to the growing mind. We chase the shadows of the coming night, Leaving the ignorance of youth behind.

Midnight Ink

The clock counts seconds like a steady heart, While liquid thoughts flow from the nib of steel. In every stanza, I tear life apart, To find the truth of everything I feel.

The Tutor’s Garden

Small hands, big questions, eyes like open doors, I plant the seeds of logic and of lore. To watch a concept bloom upon the face, Is why I run this intellectual race.

Silent Gale

The wind doesn't scream, it whispers through the leaves, A language only the lonely understand. It carries secrets that the forest weaves, Across the shifting contours of the land.

Caloocan Pulse

Concrete and neon, the rhythm of the street, Where thousand dreams and heavy footsteps meet. A city breathing in a restless haze, Finding its beauty in a metal maze.

Lexicon

Syllables stacked like bricks of ancient stone, Building a bridge to worlds I’ve never known. A single word can break a heavy chain, Or act as shelter from the biting rain.

Lunar Phase

The silver crescent hangs upon the dark, A lonely, glowing, celestial arc. We change our shapes as weeks go drifting by, But keep our glow within the heavy sky.

The Supervisor

Balance the ledger, calm the rising tide, With quiet strength and nothing left to hide. A steady hand to guide the moving parts, The science of the work, the grace of hearts.

Ambition

A tiny spark within the winter chill, That refuses to be quiet or be still. It feeds on effort, grows on steady breath, Defying failure, and the cold of death.

Epilogue

The page is white, the story has just begun, Under the gaze of the eternal sun. What’s written here is only but a trace, Of all the wonders I have yet to face.
