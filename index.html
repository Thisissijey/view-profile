Beyond the Summa Cum Laude

I am more than the titles and the academic honors. I am Christine Joy Alutaya, and this space is my breathing room. Here, I trade my English education degree for the raw, unedited language of the heart. My writing is heavily influenced by the world outside my window—the way the tide retreats by the bay, the stubborn endurance of trees, and the shifting colors of the sky at dusk.


In this creative page, I invite you to see the world through my eyes. I believe that every moment, no matter how small, deserves a stanza. This isn't just a portfolio; it's a map of my growth from 2021 to today.
